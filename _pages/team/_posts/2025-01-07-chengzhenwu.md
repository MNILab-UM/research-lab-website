Chengzhen is a first-year PhD student in the LAB. He obtained his bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering from Tianjin University and completed his master's studies at the Institute of Biomedical Engineering, Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences. He has focused on the field of neural engineering and rehabilitation, with the ambition to decode the communication patterns between the central nervous system and the peripheral nervous system.
