Danyong is a second-year PhD student in the lab. He graduated with a BPhil degree in logic from Sun Yat-sen University and a MSc degree in Cognitive Neuroscience from University of Macau. His research interests lie in the application of machine learning methods to enhance understanding of cognitive processes. Specifically, he is interested in utilizing neural networks in MRI analysis to uncover intricate patterns and relationships within brain imaging data.
