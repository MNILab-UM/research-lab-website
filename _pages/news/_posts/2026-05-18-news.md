MNI Lab members attended the **2nd Tianfu Autism Brain Science International Forum 2026 (2026年第二届天府孤独症脑科学国际论坛)**, an international meeting bringing together researchers and clinicians working at the intersection of brain science and autism spectrum disorder.

**Prof. Rihui Li** delivered an invited academic talk entitled "**Multimodal Brain Imaging to delineate Autism subtypes and its comorbidities**", presenting the lab's recent work on characterizing the heterogeneity of autism and its co-occurring conditions through multimodal neuroimaging.

We are also delighted to congratulate PhD students **Danyong Feng** and **Yuhang Li**, who were both honored with the **Best Abstract Award** for their submitted work.

Congratulations to Danyong, Yuhang, and the entire team on this wonderful recognition!
