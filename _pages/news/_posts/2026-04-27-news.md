Congratulations to MNI Lab members on their outstanding performance at the **2026 China Rehabilitation Engineering Conference (中国康复工程学术大会)**, a premier national academic meeting hosted by the Chinese Association of Rehabilitation Medicine that brings together researchers, clinicians, and engineers working on rehabilitation science and technology.

PhD student **Shuo Guan** was awarded the **Second Prize** in the poster competition, and Master's student **Yupeng Ying** was awarded the **Third Prize** in the poster competition.

We are proud of their achievements and grateful for the opportunity to share our research with the broader rehabilitation engineering community. Congratulations to both of them!
