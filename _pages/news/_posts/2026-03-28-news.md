MNI lab published a new research paper, congratulations!

The paper, entitled "**Atypical inter-brain synchrony and social communication deficits in girls with fragile X syndrome: Evidence from functional near-infrared spectroscopy hyperscanning**", was published in the journal *European Child & Adolescent Psychiatry* (IF=6.1, Q1).

**Prof. Rihui Li** is the first and corresponding author of this paper, and PhD student **Danyong Feng** is a co-author.
