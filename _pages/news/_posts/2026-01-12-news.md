MNI lab published a new research paper, congratulations!

The paper, entitled "**Enhanced Mapping of Finger Movement Representations Using Diffuse Optical Tomography: A Systematic Comparison With fNIRS**", was published in the journal *IEEE Transactions on Neural Systems and Rehabilitation Engineering* (IF=4.8, Q1).

PhD student **Shuo Guan** is the first author, and PhD student **Yuhang Li** is the second author of this paper. **Prof. Rihui Li** is the corresponding author.
